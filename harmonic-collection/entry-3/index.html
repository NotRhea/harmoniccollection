ARISTON FLOWERS AND CAFE

Rating: ★★★★☆ (4/5)

^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Ofcourse I absolutely had to try out this absolutely GORGEOUS cafe, thats nested right across Parsons. Every day I would pass this cafe and gaslight myself into believing I would eventually visit it. This weeks entry was the perfect excuse to do it! So lets get into it

AMBIANCE ★★★★☆

The cafe serves as a flower shop coupled with a coffee shop(two of my favorite things) Jokes apart, the space is actually wonderfully decorated. The blush pink seats paired with a light wood really bring out this flushed youthful vibe the cafe is going for. Having passed this cafe through all seasons of the year i can confirm its like spring all year round.

MENU★★★★☆

Ariston Flowers Cafe offers a modest yet delightful menu. The highlight here is the floral-inspired beverages and pastries. Their rose-infused latte is a must-try, exuding a subtle, floral aroma that perfectly complements the coffee. The pastries, such as the lavender macarons and hibiscus cupcakes, are equally delicious. I appreciate the uniqueness of their offerings, although a few more food options would be a welcome addition.

SERVICE★★★★☆

The staff at Ariston Flowers and Cafe were friendly and attentive, making me feel welcomed and well cared for. They were knowledgeable about both the floral and culinary aspects of the establishment, providing helpful suggestions and ensuring a pleasant dining experience.

OVERALL EXPERIENCE★★★★☆

Ariston Flowers and Cafe is a refreshing concept that brings together the beauty of nature and the pleasure of dining. While the cafe menu could offer a bit more variety, the overall experience is a delightful one. It's a perfect spot for a peaceful afternoon tea or a flower shopping excursion, and the floral workshops make it a standout destination for those interested in floral artistry.

I recommend Ariston Flowers and Cafe to anyone seeking a tranquil escape in the bustling city, a floral gift, or a unique culinary experience surrounded by natural beauty. It's a place where you can take a moment to pause and appreciate the simple joys of life amidst a sea of concrete and chaos.
